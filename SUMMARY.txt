Summary in Points:

	Dataset Source:

		Acquired the customer churn dataset from Kaggle.(https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/blastchar/telco-customer-churn/data)

	Data Cleaning:

		Performed minor data cleaning using Python in Jupyter Notebook (since the dataset was mostly clean).
		(chrun .ipynb)

	Data Analysis:

		Used Tableau to visualize the data and extract insights.
		(Book1.twb)

	Findings:

		Contract type, payment method, and tech support engagement are key factors influencing churn.
		Gender does not have a significant impact on churn.

	Conclusions:


Retention strategies can be focused on factors like contract type, payment method, and encouraging tech support usage to reduce churn.
